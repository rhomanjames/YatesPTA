Involvement

What will I do as a PTA member?

Build Relationships

Collaborate with parents, teachers and staff to foster a close school-community.

Voice Your Opinion

Let your voice be heard about suggestions and ideas that will help improve Yates.

Spark Discussions

Introduce topics that you feel will be beneficial for the school to address.

Plan Activities

Propose and organize activities to engage the school community.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the commitment?

Memberships and offices renew annually, so we encourage you to commit for at least the entire school year.

What are the leadership roles?

The President is the main leader of the organization, the treasurer manages the money and the secretary assists with organization.

How much time will it take up?

Anywhere from 1 to 6 hours a month, depending on your role. Times can also vary based on the types of events that are being organized.

What activities can we do?

Anything you feel like Yates would benefit from. Movie nights, science nights, GED classes and English classes are just a few ideas.

How much does it cost?

$4 a year. That's it!
